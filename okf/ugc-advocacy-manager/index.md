# UGC & Advocacy Manager

> A-3306 • Social Media & Community

## Overview

- **Persona:** Brand Manager
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini evaluates UGC quality beyond sentiment — assessing specificity, authenticity, and brand alignment for marketing usage. Automated monitoring surfaces customer content with real results and authentic enthusiasm. so the Brand Manager can move the UGC captured KPI.

## KPI summary

- **UGC captured**: 5-10/month manual → 50+ automated
- **Permission response rate**: 30% → 65%
- **Case study pipeline**: 2/quarter → 8/quarter
