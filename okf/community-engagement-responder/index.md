# Community Engagement Responder

> A-3303 • Social Media & Community

## Overview

- **Persona:** Social Media Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini classifies intent and drafts contextually appropriate responses — empathetic for complaints, informative for prospects. Support issues auto-routed to Zendesk, sales signals flagged in CRM without manual triage. so the Social Media Mgr can move the Avg response time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Avg response time**: 4-6 hours → < 30 minutes
- **Mentions addressed**: 40% manual → 95% automated
- **Customer satisfaction**: 3.2/5 → 4.5/5
