# Rate Indication Preparation Engine

> I-3504 • Actuarial & Portfolio Risk

## Overview

- **Persona:** Pricing Actuary
- **Department:** insurance
- **Objective:** Assembles the full indication data set — earned premium at current rate level, developed and trended losses, expense provisions — from BigQuery on request. Applies Verisk loss cost trends and prior filing selections as starting points, showing the actuary exactly what changed since the last review. so the Pricing Actuary can move the Rate indication preparation time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Rate indication preparation time**: 6 weeks → 1 week
- **Filings completed per actuary per year**: 8 → 22
- **Objection cycles per rate filing**: 2.4 → 0.9
