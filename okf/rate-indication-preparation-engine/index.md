# Rate Indication Preparation Engine

> I-3504 • Actuarial & Portfolio Risk

## Overview

- **Persona:** Pricing Actuary
- **Department:** insurance
- **Objective:** Assemble state/line rate indications from Verisk ISO ERC loss_cost_benchmarks, BigQuery earned premium and loss trend data, and prior filing selections in insurance_3_records, cutting rate indication preparation time from 6 weeks to 1 week while raising filings completed per actuary per year from 8 to 22.

## KPI summary

- **Rate indication preparation time**: 6 weeks → 1 week
- **Filings completed per actuary per year**: 8 → 22
- **Objection cycles per rate filing**: 2.4 → 0.9
