# Semantic Impact Analysis

Gemini performs meaning-level comparison — understanding that 'best efforts' to 'commercially reasonable efforts' is a commitment reduction, not a word edit. Detects deleted protections (entire Data Protection clause removed) and flags as likely intentional risk reallocation. Prioritizes changes by business impact.
