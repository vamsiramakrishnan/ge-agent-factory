# Pay Equity Audit

> A-404 • Total Rewards

## Overview

- **Persona:** CHRO
- **Department:** hr
- **Objective:** Continuous multi-variate regression on compensation data. Real-time identification of statistically significant gaps. so the CHRO can move the Audit frequency KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Audit frequency**: Annual → Continuous
- **Gap detection**: Broad averages → Regression-based
- **Remediation speed**: 6 months → Real-time
