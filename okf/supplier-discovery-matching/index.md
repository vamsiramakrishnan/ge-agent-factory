# Supplier Discovery & Matching

> A-1301 • Supplier Discovery

## Overview

- **Persona:** Sourcing Specialist
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Embedding search across multiple supplier databases matches capabilities semantically, not just by keyword. LLM translates natural-language requirements across Ariba, ThomasNet, and D&B taxonomies simultaneously. so the Sourcing Specialist can move the Supplier identification time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Supplier identification time**: 2-3 weeks → < 1 hour
- **Discovery databases queried**: 1-2 manual → 5+ simultaneous
- **Qualified candidates surfaced**: 3-5 via network → 12-20 data-matched
