# Internal Communications Drafter

> A-3506 • Brand & Communications

## Overview

- **Persona:** VP Marketing
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini drafts audience-specific variations explaining why each team should care about the announcement. LLM adapts tone for announcement type — celebratory for wins, empathetic for changes, direct for urgent. so the VP Marketing can move the Drafting time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Drafting time**: 4-6 hours → 30 minutes
- **Read rate**: 45% average → 72% with optimization
- **Audience-specific versions**: One-size-fits-all → Tailored per team
