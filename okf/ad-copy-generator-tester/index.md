# Ad Copy Generator & Tester

> A-3204 • Digital Marketing & SEO/SEM

## Overview

- **Persona:** Digital Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Ingest campaign brief, analyze historical creative performance, generate platform-tailored ad copy variants compliant with brand voice, publish to ad platforms with A/B test configuration, and recommend scaling winners based on performance data.

## KPI summary

- **Copy creation time**: 2-3 hours/campaign → 15 minutes
- **Variants tested**: 3-4 manual → 15+ automated
- **CTR improvement**: Baseline → +18% avg
