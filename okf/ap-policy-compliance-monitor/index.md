# AP Policy Compliance Monitor

> A-2207 • Accounts Payable

## Overview

- **Persona:** AP Manager
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** 100% of AP transactions scanned weekly against policy rules — no sampling gaps. Gemini detects split-purchasing patterns by analyzing same-vendor, same-day, below-threshold purchases. so the AP Manager can move the Violation detection rate KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Violation detection rate**: 30% (sample-based) → 100% (all transactions)
- **Split-purchase detection**: Rarely caught → Pattern-detected
- **Policy compliance rate**: Unknown → Scored by department
