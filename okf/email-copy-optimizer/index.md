# Email Copy Optimizer

> A-3005 \u2022 Content & Creative

## Overview

- **Persona:** Digital Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini generates 5 subject line variations testing different psychological triggers \u2014 curiosity, urgency, social proof, specificity. Adapts copy tone for different segments (enterprise vs. SMB, new lead vs. customer) based on engagement history. so the Digital Marketing Mgr can move the Subject line variants tested KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Subject line variants tested**: 2 → 5 per send
- **Average open rate lift**: Baseline → +15-25%
- **A/B test setup time**: 30 min → Automated
