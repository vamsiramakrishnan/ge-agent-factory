# Account Reconciliation Agent

> A-2103 • GL & Close

## Overview

- **Persona:** Controller
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Auto-matching and risk-based prioritization certifies 85% of accounts without manual review. Gemini reads contracts to validate prepaid and accrued balances, catching amortization errors at close. so the Controller can move the Auto-certified accounts KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Auto-certified accounts**: 20% → 85%
- **Reconciliation cycle**: 5 days → 1 day
- **Missed amortization**: Discovered at audit → Caught at close
