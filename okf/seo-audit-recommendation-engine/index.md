# SEO Audit & Recommendation Engine

> A-3201 • Digital Marketing & SEO/SEM

## Overview

- **Persona:** SEO/SEM Specialist
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini reasons about why pages underperform — diagnosing intent mismatches, not just technical issues. Automated monthly crawls with continuous ranking trend monitoring and anomaly alerts. so the SEO/SEM Specialist can move the Audit completion time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Audit completion time**: 2-3 weeks → 4 hours
- **Issues detected**: Top 20 manual → 500+ automated
- **Ranking recovery speed**: 8-12 weeks → 3-4 weeks
