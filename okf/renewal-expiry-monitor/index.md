# Renewal & Expiry Monitor

> A-1404 • Contract Lifecycle

## Overview

- **Persona:** Contract Manager
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Gemini generates renewal briefs synthesizing OTIF trends, market pricing shifts, and contract terms into actionable recommendations. LLM identifies auto-renewal traps and calculates whether cancellation windows have been missed, with alternative options. so the Contract Manager can move the Auto-renewal traps caught KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Auto-renewal traps caught**: 30% missed → 100% flagged
- **Renewal prep time**: 2-3 weeks → 1 day
- **Renegotiation savings captured**: Ad-hoc → $340K avg/renewal
