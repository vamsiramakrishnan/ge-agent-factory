# Cash Flow Forecaster

> A-2401 - Treasury & Cash

## Overview

- **Persona:** Treasurer
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Automated multi-horizon forecasting with seasonal decomposition achieves 93% accuracy at 30-day horizon. Gemini incorporates qualitative business signals that Excel models miss -- deal closings, expedite fees, one-time payments. so the Treasurer can move the Forecast accuracy (30-day) KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Forecast accuracy (30-day)**: 70-75% → 93%
- **Forecast preparation time**: 4 hours/day → Automated
- **Idle cash reduction**: $20M average → $5M average
