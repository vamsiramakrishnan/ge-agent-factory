# Content Performance Analyzer

> A-3004 \u2022 Content & Creative

## Overview

- **Persona:** Content Strategist
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini explains why high-performing blog posts lost traffic by correlating algorithm updates, competitor content changes, and freshness decay. Generates specific optimization recommendations with estimated traffic gain for each update. so the Content Strategist can move the Content decay detection KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Content decay detection**: Months later → Within 1 week
- **Optimization prioritization**: Subjective → Traffic-impact ranked
- **Content ROI visibility**: Page views only → Full-funnel attribution
