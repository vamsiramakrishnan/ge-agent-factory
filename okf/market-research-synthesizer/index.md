# Market Research Synthesizer

> A-3701 • Customer & Market Intelligence

## Overview

- **Persona:** Product Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini synthesizes 40-page Gartner reports, analyst briefings, and G2 reviews into 2-page intelligence briefs. LLM identifies non-obvious insights — shifts in buyer evaluation criteria, not just market size growth. so the Product Marketing Mgr can move the Report synthesis time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Report synthesis time**: 2-3 days per report → 2 hours
- **Sources analyzed**: 3-5 per brief → 15+ per brief
- **Stakeholder coverage**: Ad-hoc sharing → Systematic distribution
