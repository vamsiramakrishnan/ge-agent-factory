# Regulatory Filing Orchestrator

> A-2504 - Tax & Compliance

## Overview

- **Persona:** Controller
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Parallel data collection and automated validation cuts filing preparation from 4-6 weeks to 1-2 weeks. Gemini reviews disclosures against ASC requirements during assembly, catching gaps early when there is time to address them. so the Controller can move the Filing preparation time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Filing preparation time**: 4-6 weeks → 1-2 weeks
- **Disclosure gaps found**: At final review → During assembly
- **XBRL errors**: 5-10 per filing → < 1 per filing
