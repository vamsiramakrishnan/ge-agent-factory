# Competitive Battle Card Generator

> A-3705 • Customer & Market Intelligence

## Overview

- **Persona:** Product Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini generates contextual battle cards with specific objection handlers grounded in Gong call analysis. LLM references actual competitive displacements and winning messaging angles from deal data. so the Product Marketing Mgr can move the Battle card freshness KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Battle card freshness**: Updated quarterly → Continuously refreshed
- **Sales adoption**: 30% use battle cards → 85% engagement
- **Competitive win rate**: Flat baseline → +12 pts with cards
