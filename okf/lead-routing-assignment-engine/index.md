# Lead Routing & Assignment Engine

> A-3602 • Marketing Operations

## Overview

- **Persona:** Marketing Ops Lead
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini resolves ambiguous account matches by analyzing domain, LinkedIn, and engagement context. LLM determines correct routing for edge cases that rule-based systems mishandle. so the Marketing Ops Lead can move the Routing accuracy KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Routing accuracy**: 78% correct first time → 96% correct first time
- **Assignment-to-contact**: 8+ hours average → < 2 hours
- **Duplicate account creation**: 12% of leads → < 2% of leads
