# Brand Health Monitor

> A-3501 • Brand & Communications

## Overview

- **Persona:** Brand Manager
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini synthesizes mentions, search trends, sentiment, and competitive data into a unified brand health score. LLM interprets why sentiment shifted — correlating with specific events, not just reporting numbers. so the Brand Manager can move the Brand tracking cycle KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Brand tracking cycle**: Monthly manual → Real-time continuous
- **Signal sources monitored**: 2-3 platforms → 6+ platforms unified
- **Insight-to-action time**: 2-3 weeks → Same day
