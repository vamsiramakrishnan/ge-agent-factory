# Obligation Mining & Tracking

> A-1403 • Contract Lifecycle

## Overview

- **Persona:** Contract Manager
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Gemini reads 50-page contracts and extracts obligations invisible to keyword search — buried in prose, not in 'Obligations' sections. LLM distinguishes one-time deliverables from ongoing obligations and conditional triggers, creating appropriate tracking workflows. so the Contract Manager can move the Obligations captured KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Obligations captured**: 40-50% manual → 98% automated
- **Missed deadlines**: 15-20% annually → <2%
- **Extraction time per contract**: 4-8 hours → 15 minutes
