# Audience Segmentation Engine

> A-2905 \u2022 Marketing Strategy

## Overview

- **Persona:** Demand Gen Manager
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini interprets qualitative signals that scoring models miss \u2014 prospect blog posts, job postings, and LinkedIn activity signaling buying intent. K-means clustering with propensity scoring creates data-driven micro-segments beyond basic firmographics. so the Demand Gen Manager can move the Segmentation refresh KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Segmentation refresh**: Quarterly manual → Monthly automated
- **Segment personalization depth**: Firmographic only → Behavioral + intent + qualitative
- **Campaign targeting precision**: Broad personas → Data-driven micro-segments
