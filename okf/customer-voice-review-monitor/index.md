# Customer Voice & Review Monitor

> A-3703 • Customer & Market Intelligence

## Overview

- **Persona:** Product Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini extracts product intelligence from reviews — feature gaps, integration pain points, competitive signals. LLM detects patterns across reviews that individual reading misses — 'three enterprises mention same gap.' so the Product Marketing Mgr can move the Review coverage KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Review coverage**: Spot-checked weekly → 100% daily analysis
- **Negative review response**: 3-5 day lag → Same-day draft
- **Product feedback loop**: Quarterly batch → Real-time routing
