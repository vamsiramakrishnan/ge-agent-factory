# Procurement Maturity Assessor

> A-1106 • Procurement Strategy

## Overview

- **Persona:** CPO
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** NLP analyzes free-text survey responses to extract qualitative pain points alongside quantitative scoring. Benchmarks against Hackett/CAPS frameworks automatically. so the CPO can move the Assessment time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Assessment time**: 6-8 weeks → 1 week
- **Benchmark dimensions**: 5-6 manual → 25+ automated
- **Gap-to-action mapping**: None → Agent-linked
