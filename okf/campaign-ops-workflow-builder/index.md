# Campaign Ops Workflow Builder

> A-3603 • Marketing Operations

## Overview

- **Persona:** Marketing Ops Lead
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini translates natural language descriptions into MAP-specific configurations with correct branching and exclusions. LLM identifies edge cases and ambiguities upfront — asking clarifying questions before building. so the Marketing Ops Lead can move the Workflow build time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Workflow build time**: 4-8 hours → 30 minutes
- **Ops backlog**: 15-20 workflow requests → < 3 pending
- **Logic errors in production**: 1 in 5 workflows → < 1 in 20
