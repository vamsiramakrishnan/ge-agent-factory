# Intercompany Reconciliation

> A-2102 • GL & Close

## Overview

- **Persona:** GL Accountant / Controller
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Fuzzy matching across entities resolves 95% of IC transactions automatically. Gemini traces mismatches to contract amendments or unprocessed entries without manual investigation. so the GL Accountant / Controller can move the Auto-match rate KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Auto-match rate**: 60% → 95%
- **IC reconciliation time**: 3-4 days → 4 hours
- **Unresolved items at close**: 15-20 → 2-3
