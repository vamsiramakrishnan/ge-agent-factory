# Onboarding Effectiveness Analyzer

> A-213 • Onboarding & Integration

## Overview

- **Persona:** HR Ops Lead
- **Department:** hr
- **Objective:** Multi-signal effectiveness scoring combining survey sentiment, early performance metrics, and engagement data. Cohort benchmarking that compares onboarding outcomes across roles, locations, and hiring channels. so the HR Ops Lead can move the Measurement depth KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Measurement depth**: Completion rate only → Multi-signal
- **Attrition prediction**: None → 30-day forecast
- **Cohort benchmarking**: Annual → Real-time
