# UTM & Tracking Governance Agent

> A-3606 • Marketing Operations

## Overview

- **Persona:** Marketing Ops Lead
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini detects semantic duplicates that pattern matching misses — different word orders referring to same campaign. LLM suggests canonical naming and identifies historical attribution data needing cleanup. so the Marketing Ops Lead can move the UTM compliance rate KPI.

## KPI summary

- **UTM compliance rate**: 62% of campaigns → 98% of campaigns
- **Attribution accuracy**: Fragmented by naming → Unified canonical
- **Manual URL review**: 15 min/campaign → Automated
