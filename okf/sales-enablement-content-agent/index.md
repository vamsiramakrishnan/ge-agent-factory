# Sales Enablement Content Agent

> A-3706 • Customer & Market Intelligence

## Overview

- **Persona:** Product Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini recommends the right content at the right deal moment based on context, not just stage. LLM provides personalization angles — 'the CTO's LinkedIn shows API-first interests, lead with that angle.' so the Product Marketing Mgr can move the Content find time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Content find time**: 15-20 min searching → Proactively pushed
- **Content utilization**: 35% of library used → 78% relevant usage
- **Win influence**: Untracked → Content-attributed wins tracked
