# Payment Optimization Agent

> A-1507 • Procure-to-Pay

## Overview

- **Persona:** Treasury
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Dynamic discounting engine evaluates early pay discount APR vs. company cost of capital for every invoice. Resolves edge cases: '2/10 Net 30 vs. supply chain finance at LIBOR+200bps — which is more cost-effective?' with full scenario modeling. so the Treasury can move the Early pay discount capture KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Early pay discount capture**: 12% of eligible → 78% of eligible
- **Annual discount revenue**: $340K → $2.1M
- **Working capital impact**: Unoptimized DPO → +4 days DPO
