# Category Strategy Generator

> A-1101 • Procurement Strategy

## Overview

- **Persona:** Category Director
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Gemini synthesizes 3 years of spend, market intel, and supplier performance into coherent strategy narratives. LLM reasons about which savings levers are realistic given category maturity. so the Category Director can move the Strategy creation time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Strategy creation time**: 4-6 weeks → 2 days
- **Data sources analyzed**: 3-4 manual → 15+ automated
- **Savings pipeline accuracy**: Gut feel → Data-validated
