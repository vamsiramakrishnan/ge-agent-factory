# Bank Reconciliation Agent

> A-2402 - Treasury & Cash

## Overview

- **Persona:** Treasury Accountant
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** ML matching achieves 96% auto-reconciliation rate, reducing manual effort from 3 hours to 20 minutes of exception review. Gemini investigates unmatched items by reading bank descriptions and cross-referencing internal records -- no phone calls needed. so the Treasury Accountant can move the Auto-match rate KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Auto-match rate**: 70% → 96%
- **Reconciliation time**: 3 hours/day → 20 min review
- **Unresolved items aging**: 5-10 days → 1-2 days
