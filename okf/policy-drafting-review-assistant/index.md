# Policy Drafting & Review Assistant

> A-606 • Employee Relations

## Overview

- **Persona:** ER Lead
- **Department:** hr
- **Objective:** Regulation-aware policy drafting from curated templates aligned to current law. Automated compliance gap analysis flagging conflicts with federal, state, and local regulations. so the ER Lead can move the Draft time KPI.
