# Procurement Policy Assistant

> A-1104 • Procurement Strategy

## Overview

- **Persona:** All Procurement Staff
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** RAG over procurement policies with conversational Q&A and direct citations. LLM navigates complex delegation of authority rules — 'software under $25K with an existing EA is exempt from competitive bidding.' so the All Procurement Staff can move the Policy query resolution KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Policy query resolution**: Email to policy team → Instant chat
- **Query accuracy**: Varies → 95%+ with citations
- **Support tickets**: 200+/month → <30/month
