# Transfer Pricing Monitor

> A-2502 - Tax & Compliance

## Overview

- **Persona:** Tax Director
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Continuous quarterly monitoring catches out-of-range pricing before audit exposure, enabling proactive adjustment. Gemini interprets OECD guidelines and generates value-add documentation, reducing reliance on external advisors. so the Tax Director can move the Monitoring frequency KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Monitoring frequency**: Annual review → Continuous quarterly
- **Out-of-range transactions**: Discovered in audit → Proactive adjustment
- **Documentation time**: 6-8 weeks/year → Auto-generated quarterly
