# GTM Launch Planner

> A-2904 \u2022 Marketing Strategy

## Overview

- **Persona:** VP Marketing
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini interprets product requirements to generate tailored launch checklists appropriate for the launch tier. LLM reasons about which channels and tactics are appropriate for different launch types based on historical performance. so the VP Marketing can move the Launch plan creation KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Launch plan creation**: 2-3 weeks → 1 day
- **Cross-functional alignment**: 5+ meetings → Auto-orchestrated
- **Launch readiness score**: Subjective → Data-driven checklist
