# Market Trend & Signal Detector

> A-3707 • Customer & Market Intelligence

## Overview

- **Persona:** Marketing Analyst
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini distinguishes meaningful market shifts from noise by corroborating search trends with adoption signals. LLM assesses whether search volume spikes indicate real buyer activity or media-driven curiosity. so the Marketing Analyst can move the Trend detection speed KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Trend detection speed**: Weeks behind market → < 7 day detection
- **Signal accuracy**: Chasing hype cycles → Validated with corroboration
- **Content response time**: 60-90 days → < 30 days from detection
