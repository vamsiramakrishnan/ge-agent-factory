# Supplier Pre-Qualification Screener

> A-1302 • Supplier Discovery

## Overview

- **Persona:** Sourcing Specialist
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** NLP extraction reads full narrative answers — 'currently pursuing ISO 13485 with expected Q4 completion' flagged as conditional. LLM evaluates narrative responses for credibility and cross-references against D&B data and supporting documents. so the Sourcing Specialist can move the Screening turnaround KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Screening turnaround**: 5-7 business days → < 4 hours
- **Questionnaire review depth**: Checkbox scan → Full narrative analysis
- **Inconsistency detection rate**: ~30% caught → 95%+ flagged
