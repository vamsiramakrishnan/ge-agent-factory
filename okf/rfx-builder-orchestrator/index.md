# RFx Builder & Orchestrator

> A-1204 • Strategic Sourcing

## Overview

- **Persona:** Strategic Sourcing Lead
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Gemini reads a 15-page requirements document and drafts tailored evaluation criteria — adapting language to category complexity. LLM generates contextual clarification questions when supplier responses are ambiguous about uptime, SLAs, or capacity commitments. so the Strategic Sourcing Lead can move the RFP creation time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **RFP creation time**: 2-3 weeks → 2 days
- **Supplier Q&A turnaround**: 3-5 days → Same day
- **Bid response rate**: 60% → 85%
