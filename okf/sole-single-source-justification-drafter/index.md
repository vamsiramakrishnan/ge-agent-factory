# Sole/Single Source Justification Drafter

> A-1209 • Strategic Sourcing

## Overview

- **Persona:** CPO
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Gemini drafts justifications that meet audit requirements — reasoning through proprietary lock-in, qualification timelines, and regulatory barriers. LLM cross-checks claims against market data: if 4 suppliers have the capability, the agent challenges the sole-source assertion before it reaches the CPO. so the CPO can move the Justification draft time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Justification draft time**: 2-3 days → 2 hours
- **Audit finding rate**: 15% of justifications → <3%
- **False sole-source claims caught**: Rarely challenged → 28% redirected to competitive bid
