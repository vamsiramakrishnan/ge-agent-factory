# Campaign Builder & Orchestrator

> A-3101 \u2022 Demand Generation

## Overview

- **Persona:** Demand Gen Manager
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini interprets campaign briefs and reasons about the optimal multi-touch journey for each audience segment. Drafts personalized email sequences with contextually different follow-ups based on engagement signals and buying intent. so the Demand Gen Manager can move the Campaign setup time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Campaign setup time**: 3-5 days → 4 hours
- **Email sequence quality**: Template-based → Contextually personalized
- **Multi-channel coordination**: Manual → Automated orchestration
