# Scenario Modeling & Sensitivity

> A-2004 • FP&A

## Overview

- **Persona:** CFO / FP&A Director
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Natural-language what-if questions translated directly into simulation parameters by Gemini. Monte Carlo runs across 20+ variables in minutes with full probability distributions. so the CFO / FP&A Director can move the Scenario turnaround KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Scenario turnaround**: 1-2 weeks → Minutes
- **Variables modeled**: 3-5 manual → 20+ simultaneous
- **Decision confidence**: Single-point estimate → Probability distribution
