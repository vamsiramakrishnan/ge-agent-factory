# Resume Screening & Shortlisting

> A-204 • Talent Acquisition

## Overview

- **Persona:** Recruiter
- **Department:** hr
- **Objective:** Deep reasoning over resumes, portfolios, and GitHub profiles. Ranked shortlists based on competency-to-role mapping. so the Recruiter can move the Screening time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Screening time**: 8 min/resume → 5 sec
- **Bias incidents**: Undetected → Flagged
- **Quality of shortlist**: 60% relevant → 92% relevant
