# Onboarding Orchestration

> A-211 • Talent Acquisition

## Overview

- **Persona:** HR Ops Lead
- **Department:** hr
- **Objective:** Multi-step orchestration of IT, Facilities, and HR tasks via API. Auto-assignment of training by role, location, and seniority. so the HR Ops Lead can move the Time to productivity KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Time to productivity**: 45 days → 21 days
- **Compliance completion**: Week 2 → Day 1
- **90-day retention**: 82% → 94%
