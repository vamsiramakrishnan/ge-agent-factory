# Benefits Q&A & Enrollment

> A-406 • Total Rewards

## Overview

- **Persona:** Employee
- **Department:** hr
- **Objective:** Answer a benefits question with personalized plan evidence and, when an employee asks to enroll or change coverage, submit the enrollment to the Benefits Platform and confirm via Google Chat — never invent enrollments, plan IDs, or carrier sync results.

## KPI summary

- **Response time**: 24 hrs → Instant
- **HR ticket reduction**: Baseline → -55%
- **Enrollment accuracy**: 85% → 98%
