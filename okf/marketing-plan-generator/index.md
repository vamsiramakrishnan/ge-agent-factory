# Marketing Plan Generator

> A-2901 \u2022 Marketing Strategy

## Overview

- **Persona:** CMO
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini synthesizes pipeline data, campaign performance, and competitive landscape into a coherent plan narrative with strategic rationale. LLM reasons about which channels deserve incremental investment vs. sunset based on diminishing returns curves and market shifts. so the CMO can move the Plan creation time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Plan creation time**: 3-4 weeks → 2 days
- **Data sources synthesized**: 3-4 manual → 12+ automated
- **Channel allocation accuracy**: Gut feel → Model-validated
