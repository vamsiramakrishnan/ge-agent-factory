# Sales & Use Tax Automation

> A-2503 - Tax & Compliance

## Overview

- **Persona:** Tax Analyst
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Tax engine handles 99%+ of transactions automatically; Gemini resolves the remaining ambiguous edge cases. LLM interprets state-specific rules for SaaS taxability, manufacturing exemptions, and bundled service treatment. so the Tax Analyst can move the Tax calculation accuracy KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Tax calculation accuracy**: 94% → 99.8%
- **Manual tax determinations**: 200/month → 10/month (edge cases)
- **Audit assessment risk**: $500K+ exposure → < $50K
