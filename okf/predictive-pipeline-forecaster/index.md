# Predictive Pipeline Forecaster

> A-3407 • Marketing Analytics & Attribution

## Overview

- **Persona:** Marketing Analyst
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini provides forecast narratives with confidence levels, specific deal risks, and intent-signal upside opportunities. Predictive deal scoring enriched with marketing engagement and 6sense intent data. so the Marketing Analyst can move the Forecast accuracy KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Forecast accuracy**: ±30% manual → ±10% predictive
- **Risk identification**: End of quarter → Weekly proactive
- **Upside detection**: Anecdotal → Intent-signal based
