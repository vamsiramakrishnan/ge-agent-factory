# Market Intelligence Monitor

> A-1202 • Strategic Sourcing

## Overview

- **Persona:** Category Manager
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Gemini reads a Financial Times article on rare earth tariffs and reasons that 3 of your electronics suppliers source sub-components from affected regions. LLM synthesizes commodity data, news, and supplier geography into briefs: 'Steel up 8% MoM from EU CBAM — accelerate Q3 structural components sourcing.' so the Category Manager can move the Market signal latency KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Market signal latency**: Days to weeks → <4 hours
- **Sources monitored**: 2-3 manual → 40+ automated
- **Actionable alerts/month**: Ad hoc → 12-15 validated
