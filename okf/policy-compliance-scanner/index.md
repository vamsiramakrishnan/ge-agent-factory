# Policy Compliance Scanner

> A-2605 • Internal Audit & Controls

## Overview

- **Persona:** Chief Audit Executive
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** 100% transaction scanning replaces manual sampling for complete coverage. Gemini interprets exceptions in context — high-cost city travel vs. unjustified overspend. so the Chief Audit Executive can move the Transactions scanned KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Transactions scanned**: 5-10% sample → 100% population
- **False positive rate**: 50-60% → < 20%
- **Policy violation trend visibility**: Annual review → Quarterly with trends
