# Intercompany Netting Agent

> A-2406 - Treasury & Cash

## Overview

- **Persona:** Treasury Analyst
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Multi-currency netting optimization reduces 120 payments to 15 net settlements, cutting bank fees and processing time by 85%. Gemini interprets withholding tax treaties and regulatory exceptions automatically, ensuring compliance with audit trail. so the Treasury Analyst can move the Settlement count reduction KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Settlement count reduction**: 120 payments/month → 15 net settlements
- **FX conversion savings**: Gross conversion → $800K/year saved
- **Withholding tax accuracy**: Manual treaty lookup → Automated with audit trail
