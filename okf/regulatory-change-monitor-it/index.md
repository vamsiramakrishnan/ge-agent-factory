# Regulatory Change Monitor

> A-4506 • IT Governance & Compliance

## Overview

- **Persona:** Compliance & GRC Lead
- **Department:** it
- **Objective:** Gemini scans regulatory feeds weekly and filters for changes relevant to the organization's IT operations. LLM maps new requirements to existing controls, distinguishing between major gaps and minor policy updates. so the Compliance & GRC Lead can move the Regulatory change awareness KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Regulatory change awareness**: Reactive discovery → Proactive weekly scan
- **Impact assessment time**: 2-3 weeks → 2-3 days
- **Compliance deadline misses**: 3-4 per year → Zero
