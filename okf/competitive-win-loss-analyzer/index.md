# Competitive Win-Loss Analyzer

> T-4104 • Sales & Customer Acquisition

## Overview

- **Persona:** Sales Operations Analyst
- **Department:** telco
- **Objective:** The analyzer classifies every closed opportunity by loss driver from CRM notes, call summaries, and quote history. It detects emerging competitor pricing moves by market and product line within a weekly cadence. so the Sales Operations Analyst can move the Win-loss analysis cycle KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Win-loss analysis cycle**: quarterly, 3-week effort → weekly, automated
- **Competitive win rate in contested deals**: 38% → 47%
- **Opportunities with loss reason coded**: 45% → 96%
