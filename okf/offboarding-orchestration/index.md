# Offboarding Orchestration

> A-807 • HR Operations

## Overview

- **Persona:** HR Ops Lead
- **Department:** hr
- **Objective:** Autonomous orchestration of deprovisioning and asset recovery. Automated compliance notice generation (COBRA, etc.). so the HR Ops Lead can move the Deprovisioning time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Deprovisioning time**: 5 days → 4 hours
