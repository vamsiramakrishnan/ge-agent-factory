# ESG & Sustainability Reporter

> A-2807 • Finance Analytics & Reporting

## Overview

- **Persona:** CFO
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Automated data pipelines replace manual surveys for emissions, energy, and social metrics. Gemini drafts disclosures simultaneously mapped to GRI, SASB, and TCFD frameworks. so the CFO can move the Report preparation KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Report preparation**: 8-12 weeks → 3-4 weeks
- **Framework coverage**: 1-2 frameworks → GRI + SASB + TCFD
- **Data collection effort**: Manual surveys → Automated pipeline
