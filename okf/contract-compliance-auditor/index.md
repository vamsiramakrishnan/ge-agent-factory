# Contract Compliance Auditor

> A-1406 • Contract Lifecycle

## Overview

- **Persona:** Contract Manager
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Monthly audit of contract terms against actuals: match pricing schedules and invoices, detect overcharges and rebate cliff opportunities, interpret complex index-based formulas with dead bands and caps, and generate exception reports with citations and financial impact.

## KPI summary

- **Pricing overcharges detected**: Spot-checked quarterly → 100% monthly
- **Rebate revenue recovered**: $0 (thresholds missed) → $180K avg/quarter
- **Compliance audit time**: 2 weeks manual → Automated monthly
