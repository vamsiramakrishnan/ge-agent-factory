# Crisis Communications Advisor

> A-3503 • Brand & Communications

## Overview

- **Persona:** CMO
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini detects crisis signals in real-time and distinguishes genuine threats from manufactured controversy. LLM generates tiered response strategy calibrated to severity — not every negative mention is a crisis. so the CMO can move the Crisis detection time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Crisis detection time**: Hours to days → < 15 minutes
- **First response time**: 4-8 hours → < 1 hour
- **Response consistency**: Ad-hoc per channel → Coordinated cross-channel
