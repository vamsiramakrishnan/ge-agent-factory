# Brand Guidelines Enforcer

> A-3505 • Brand & Communications

## Overview

- **Persona:** Brand Manager
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini scans every submitted asset for visual and messaging compliance in under 5 minutes. LLM catches tone mismatches that pixel-level checks miss — fear-based language conflicting with empowering brand voice. so the Brand Manager can move the Review turnaround KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Review turnaround**: 2-3 days manual → < 5 minutes
- **Violation catch rate**: ~60% spot-checked → 100% automated scan
- **Brand consistency score**: Untracked → 92%+ across assets
