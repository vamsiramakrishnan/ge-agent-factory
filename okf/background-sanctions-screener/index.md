# Background & Sanctions Screener

> A-1308 • Supplier Discovery

## Overview

- **Persona:** Compliance Manager
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Fuzzy name matching with entity context resolves 90%+ of ambiguous matches automatically — 'Mohammad Al-Hassan' matched against 47 entries by country, industry, and associated entities. LLM distinguishes relevant adverse media from noise — bribery investigation in Nigeria is critical, charity sponsorship is not. so the Compliance Manager can move the Screening turnaround KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Screening turnaround**: 3-5 business days → < 2 hours
- **False positive resolution**: Manual review of all hits → 90%+ auto-adjudicated
- **Beneficial owner tracing**: Not performed → Systematic entity relationship mapping
