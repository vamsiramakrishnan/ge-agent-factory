# Creative Asset Generator

> A-3003 \u2022 Content & Creative

## Overview

- **Persona:** Brand Manager
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini interprets campaign briefs to generate contextually appropriate ad copy and messaging variations aligned with brand guidelines. Reasons about which messaging angle will resonate based on target audience segment and funnel stage. so the Brand Manager can move the Asset creation time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Asset creation time**: 2-3 days → 2 hours
- **Variations per campaign**: 3-4 → 10-15
- **Brand compliance rate**: 78% → 95%
