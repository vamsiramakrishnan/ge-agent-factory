# Email Deliverability Manager

> A-3605 • Marketing Operations

## Overview

- **Persona:** Marketing Ops Lead
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini correlates deliverability drops with specific sends, segments, and content to diagnose root causes. LLM distinguishes between domain reputation damage, content triggers, and list hygiene issues. so the Marketing Ops Lead can move the Inbox placement rate KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Inbox placement rate**: 82% average → 94% sustained
- **Issue detection to fix**: 3-5 days → Same day
- **Domain reputation incidents**: 2-3 per quarter → < 1 per quarter
