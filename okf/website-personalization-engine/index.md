# Website Personalization Engine

> A-3205 • Digital Marketing & SEO/SEM

## Overview

- **Persona:** Digital Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini reasons about multi-signal personalization — combining firmographic, behavioral, and intent data for contextual experiences. Real-time visitor classification with account-level matching and propensity scoring. so the Digital Marketing Mgr can move the Conversion rate lift KPI.

- **Conversion rate lift**: Generic pages → +35% personalized
- **Personalization coverage**: 2 segments → 12+ segments
