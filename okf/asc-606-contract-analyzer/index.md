# ASC 606 Contract Analyzer

> A-2705 • Revenue & Cost Accounting

## Overview

- **Persona:** Controller
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Gemini reads complex contract language and applies the 5-step ASC 606 model with cited guidance. Precedent matching ensures consistent treatment across similar contracts. so the Controller can move the Contract analysis time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Contract analysis time**: 1-2 days → 2-3 hours
- **Consistency across analysts**: Variable treatment → Standardized with precedent matching
- **Audit readiness**: Memos created ad-hoc → Auto-generated with cited guidance
