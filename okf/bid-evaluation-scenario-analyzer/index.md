# Bid Evaluation & Scenario Analyzer

> A-1205 • Strategic Sourcing

## Overview

- **Persona:** Sourcing Committee
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Gemini synthesizes 6 bids across 40 criteria into a narrative that explains why Supplier B's higher price is justified by quality track record and capacity headroom. LLM reads narrative responses to assess credibility: distinguishing marketing claims from substantiated capability commitments. so the Sourcing Committee can move the Evaluation cycle time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Evaluation cycle time**: 2-3 weeks → 3 days
- **Award scenarios modeled**: 2-3 manual → 20+ automated
- **Total value captured**: Price-focused → Multi-dimensional TCO
