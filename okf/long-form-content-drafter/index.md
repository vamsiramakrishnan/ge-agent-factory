# Long-Form Content Drafter

> A-3002 \u2022 Content & Creative

## Overview

- **Persona:** Content Strategist
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini generates domain-expert-quality drafts with original analysis, relevant examples, and clear narrative arcs. Maintains brand voice while adapting tone to content type \u2014 thought leadership vs. how-to vs. comparison guide. so the Content Strategist can move the First draft time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **First draft time**: 3-5 days → 2 hours
- **Revision cycles**: 3-4 rounds → 1-2 rounds
- **Content output volume**: 4-6 pieces/month → 12-15 pieces/month
