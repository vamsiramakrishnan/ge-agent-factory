# Audit Evidence Collector

> A-4502 • IT Governance & Compliance

## Overview

- **Persona:** Compliance & GRC Lead
- **Department:** it
- **Objective:** Gemini interprets auditor requests in audit language and maps them to specific internal evidence sources. Auto-collects evidence from Jira, Google Drive, Slack, and ServiceNow — eliminating manual artifact hunting. so the Compliance & GRC Lead can move the Evidence collection time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Evidence collection time**: 3-4 weeks per audit → 2-3 days
- **Evidence completeness**: 70% on first pass → 92% auto-collected
- **Audit prep labor hours**: 200+ hours → 40 hours
