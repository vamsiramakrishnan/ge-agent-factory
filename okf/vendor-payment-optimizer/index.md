# Vendor Payment Optimizer

> A-2202 • Accounts Payable

## Overview

- **Persona:** AP Manager / Treasurer
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Dynamic payment optimization balances DPO targets against discount APR and cash availability. Gemini evaluates complex scenarios where discounts compete with supply chain finance programs. so the AP Manager / Treasurer can move the Discount capture rate KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Discount capture rate**: 35% → 92%
- **Working capital freed**: Baseline → $5M+ annually
- **DPO optimization**: Fixed terms → Dynamic by vendor
