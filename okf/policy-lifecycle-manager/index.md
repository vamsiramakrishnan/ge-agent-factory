# Policy Lifecycle Manager

> A-4503 • IT Governance & Compliance

## Overview

- **Persona:** Compliance & GRC Lead
- **Department:** it
- **Objective:** Gemini monitors review dates and regulatory changes to trigger timely policy reviews automatically. LLM drafts policy updates that incorporate regulatory requirements, incident learnings, and industry standards. so the Compliance & GRC Lead can move the Overdue policy reviews KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Overdue policy reviews**: 35% past due → < 5% past due
- **Policy update cycle**: 4-6 weeks → 1 week
- **Employee acknowledgment rate**: 62% → 94%
