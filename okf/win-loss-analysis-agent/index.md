# Win/Loss Analysis Agent

> A-3702 • Customer & Market Intelligence

## Overview

- **Persona:** Product Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini analyzes Gong transcripts to surface real loss reasons — product gaps hidden behind 'lost on price' dropdown. LLM detects patterns across deals that individual analysis misses — 4 of last 7 enterprise losses share same gap. so the Product Marketing Mgr can move the Analysis per deal KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Analysis per deal**: 10% of deals analyzed → 100% auto-analyzed
- **True loss reason accuracy**: CRM dropdowns unreliable → Transcript-verified
- **Insight-to-action cycle**: Quarterly batch review → Real-time per deal
