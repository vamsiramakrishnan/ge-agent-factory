# Lead Nurture Optimizer

> A-3106 \u2022 Demand Generation

## Overview

- **Persona:** Demand Gen Manager
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini diagnoses why leads stall in specific sequence positions \u2014 content mismatch, premature ask, or timing issues. Generates adaptive nurture content that responds to behavioral signals rather than following rigid branching logic. so the Demand Gen Manager can move the Nurture-to-MQL rate KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Nurture-to-MQL rate**: 12% → 24%
- **Sequence stall detection**: End-of-quarter review → Weekly automated
- **Re-engagement success**: 5% → 18%
