# Campaign Calendar Orchestrator

> A-2906 \u2022 Marketing Strategy

## Overview

- **Persona:** VP Marketing
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini reasons about campaign priority when overlapping audiences are targeted in the same week, considering funnel stage and deal potential. Automated audience overlap detection prevents response fatigue from concurrent campaigns. so the VP Marketing can move the Scheduling conflicts detected KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Scheduling conflicts detected**: Post-launch → Pre-launch automated
- **Audience overlap resolution**: Manual review → AI-prioritized
- **Campaign coordination time**: 3-4 hours/week → 30 min/week
