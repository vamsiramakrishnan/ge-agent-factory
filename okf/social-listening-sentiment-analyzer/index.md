# Social Listening & Sentiment Analyzer

> A-3302 • Social Media & Community

## Overview

- **Persona:** Social Media Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini interprets nuanced social conversations — distinguishing genuine criticism from manufactured controversy. Real-time anomaly detection alerts on sentiment spikes before they become crises. so the Social Media Mgr can move the Sentiment detection KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Sentiment detection**: Weekly manual → Real-time
- **Crisis response time**: 24-48 hours → < 2 hours
- **Share of voice accuracy**: Estimated → Continuous tracking
