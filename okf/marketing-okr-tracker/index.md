# Marketing OKR Tracker

> A-2907 \u2022 Marketing Strategy

## Overview

- **Persona:** CMO
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini interprets why specific OKRs are off-track by correlating pipeline, campaign, and engagement data with external factors. Trajectory forecasting with confidence intervals predicts quarter-end achievement probability for each KR. so the CMO can move the OKR reporting time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **OKR reporting time**: 4-6 hours/week → Automated
- **Forecast accuracy**: \u00b130% → \u00b110%
- **Gap diagnosis depth**: Surface metrics → Multi-source root cause
