# Persona & ICP Refiner

> A-3704 • Customer & Market Intelligence

## Overview

- **Persona:** Product Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini discovers ICP signals beyond firmographics — digital maturity, hiring patterns, technology adoption. LLM generates persona narratives grounded in actual buyer behavior, not marketing assumptions. so the Product Marketing Mgr can move the ICP update frequency KPI.

## KPI summary

- **ICP update frequency**: Annual manual refresh → Quarterly data-driven
- **Win rate in ICP segment**: 24% average → 38% with refined ICP
- **Attributes in model**: Size + Industry → 12+ signals including maturity
