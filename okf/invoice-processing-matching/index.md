# Invoice Processing & Matching

> A-2201 • Accounts Payable

## Overview

- **Persona:** AP Manager
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Match an invoice to purchase order and goods receipt, resolve matching exceptions via Gemini, post to SAP, and document all decisions with source evidence and policy citations.

## KPI summary

- **Touchless processing rate**: 60% → 95%+
- **Exception resolution time**: 4 hours → 15 minutes
- **Invoice processing cost**: $12/invoice → $2/invoice
