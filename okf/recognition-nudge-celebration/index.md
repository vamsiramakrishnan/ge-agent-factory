> A-705 • Employee Engagement

## Overview

- **Persona:** Manager
- **Department:** hr
- **Objective:** Proactive nudges to managers with low recognition activity. Auto-generated milestone celebrations and personalized notes. so the Manager can move the Recognition frequency KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Recognition frequency**: Sporadic → 3x increase
