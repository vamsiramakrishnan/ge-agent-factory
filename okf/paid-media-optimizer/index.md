# Paid Media Optimizer

> A-3104 \u2022 Demand Generation

## Overview

- **Persona:** Digital Marketing Mgr
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Gemini diagnoses why campaign performance degraded by reading ad copy, landing pages, and competitive landscape for root cause analysis. Creative fatigue detection identifies saturation within 3 days, enabling proactive creative rotation. so the Digital Marketing Mgr can move the ROAS improvement KPI.

## KPI summary

- **ROAS improvement**: Baseline → +25-40%
- **Optimization frequency**: Weekly manual → Daily automated
- **Creative fatigue detection**: Weeks late → < 3 days
