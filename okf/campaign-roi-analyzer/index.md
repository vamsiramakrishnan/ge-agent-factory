# Campaign ROI Analyzer

> A-3108 \u2022 Demand Generation

## Overview

- **Persona:** VP Marketing
- **Department:** marketing
- **Objective:** Generate multi-touch attribution results with calculated CAC per campaign, deliver executive ROI narrative with investment reallocation recommendations, and refresh Looker dashboards — all grounded in Salesforce pipeline + spend evidence with no CAC invention.

- **ROI reporting time**: 1-2 weeks → Automated weekly
