# Architecture Compliance Scanner

> A-4406 • Enterprise Architecture

## Overview

- **Persona:** Enterprise Architect
- **Department:** it
- **Objective:** Gemini scans code, infrastructure, and runtime traces weekly against architecture guardrails. LLM explains violations with business context — why the guardrail matters and what risk the violation creates. so the Enterprise Architect can move the Architecture drift detection KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Architecture drift detection**: Quarterly manual review → Continuous weekly
- **Guardrail compliance**: 68% → 92%
- **Violation remediation time**: 45 days average → 12 days average
