# Clause Risk Analyzer

> A-1402 • Contract Lifecycle

## Overview

- **Persona:** Legal Procurement Counsel
- **Department:** procurement
- **Objective:** Gemini understands that uncapped 'consequential damages' combined with broad indemnification creates compounding liability exposure. LLM detects semantic deviations — recognizing that 'commercially reasonable efforts' is a meaningful reduction from 'best efforts'. so the Legal Procurement Counsel can move the Risk detection rate KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Risk detection rate**: 60% manual review → 95% automated
- **Review turnaround**: 3-5 business days → 4 hours
- **Cross-clause risks caught**: Rarely identified → 100% flagged
