# Tax Audit Prep Agent

> A-2507 - Tax & Compliance

## Overview

- **Persona:** Tax Director
- **Department:** finance
- **Objective:** Automated document collection reduces preparation from 2-3 weeks to 2-3 days with organized, indexed packages. Gemini drafts responses citing specific regulatory sections -- Reg. 1.41-4(a) for R&D credits -- with project-level evidence. so the Tax Director can move the Document collection time KPI.

## KPI summary

- **Document collection time**: 2-3 weeks → 2-3 days
- **Response drafting**: 1 week per topic → 1-2 days with review
- **Audit assessment outcomes**: Reactive concessions → Evidence-based defense
